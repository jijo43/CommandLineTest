FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge an hourly rate?

I believe the best output happens outside an hourly rate. I deliver stellar output regardless of the hours I use to work on the project. 

What is your work process?

When you decide it’s time to work with me, here’s what you can expect:

  1. We hop in on a 30-min discovery call where I learn about your exact needs and your business. We also meet each other and decide if there’s a good fit. Prior to the call, I’ll send over a set of discovery call questions that you can answer during or prior to the call. 
  2. I will prepare an outline or strategy document based on our call and send it within 1-2 days. 
  3. You will decide if the outline works and if you’re happy, I’d ask you to settle the 50% upfront payment.
  4. Once payment is settled, I begin the writing process. I’ll flesh out the outline and create my first draft. This usually takes 5-7 days depending on the topic’s complexity. For case studies and white papers, the process takes 8-10 days. 
  5. I will send you the initial draft via Google Docs and wait for your comments. This process reduces the need for revisions once the final draft is submitted.
  6. After you make your comments, I will improve the first draft and proceed to finish the writing project. 
  7. Images are submitted along with the final draft. 
  8. When you’re 100% happy with the final draft, I send over the final billing and get paid.
How many revisions are allowed?

Once the final draft is submitted, I only allow 2 revisions maximum. I create a first draft (which will be very close to the final draft) for your comments and inputs. This reduces the time needed for lengthy revisions.

I reserve the right to reject revisions which would force me to create a completely new article from the original outline approved prior to the engagement.

Do you offer ghostwriting services?

All content you order from me is required to carry my byline. This helps build my portfolio and organic links to my own website for SEO purposes. However, if you want ghostwritten content, simply add 20% to the project price.

I’m a small start-up business. Can I negotiate the rates?

While my rates are one of the lowest in the industry, I understand where you’re coming from. I’m willing to discuss other options during our discovery call. So don’t hesitate to book a call with me! After all, the discovery call is free.

What are your payment methods?

I accept payment through PayPal only at this point. I will send you a PayPal invoice for every project milestone
